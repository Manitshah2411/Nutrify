Our Features

Everything You Need for a Healthier School

Easy Meal Planning

School administrators can create and publish daily meal plans in minutes using a pre-approved, Jain-friendly food database.

Instant Access for Parents

Students and parents can log in to view the day's menu, including nutritional information, from any device.

Secure & Private

With a closed registration system, only verified students from your school can access the platform, ensuring privacy.
